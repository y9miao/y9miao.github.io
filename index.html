My name is Yang Miao (缪洋). I am currently a master student in Robotics, System and Control at ETH Zurich. I obtained my Bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation at Harbin Institute of Technology.

My research interests include Robotics and Compter Vision, specifically, 3D reconstruction, Scene Understanding and SLAM. Currently, I work as a research assistant on Visual Localization on 3D Scene Graph at CVG Lab, supervised by Prof. Dr. Marc Pollefeys, Dr. Francis Engelmann, Dr. Olga Vysotska and Dr. Dániel Béla Baráth. Previously, I took my Master thesis on Consistent Panoptic SLAM. at CVG Lab, supervised by Dr. Iro Armeni and Dr. Dániel Béla Baráth. I aslo did my Semester Project on Visual Odometry with New Unprecedented Event Camera. at RPG Lab, supervised by Nico Messikommer and Dr. Daniel Gehrig.
